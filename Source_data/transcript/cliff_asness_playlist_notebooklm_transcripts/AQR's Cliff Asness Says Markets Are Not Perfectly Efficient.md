# AQR's Cliff Asness Says Markets Are Not Perfectly Efficient

[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=SCyTLZIR-8c)

## Transcript

In these interviews, AQR Capital Management’s Cliff Asness addresses the difficulties of investing when both stocks and bonds are historically expensive, leading to lower anticipated future returns. He discourages investors from attempting to time the market, suggesting instead that they diversify globally and consider alternative assets like commodities to mitigate risks. Asness highlights the strategic utility of derivatives and leverage to balance portfolios, provided they are used to manage risk rather than simply amplify small gains. He also criticizes the traditional hedge fund fee structure, arguing that investors should not pay premium prices for simple, widely known strategies that lack true alpha. To navigate this low-return environment, he advocates for transparent pricing and professional guidance to ensure that investment products align with long-term financial goals.
