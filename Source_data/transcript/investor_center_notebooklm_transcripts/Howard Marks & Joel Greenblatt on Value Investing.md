# Howard Marks & Joel Greenblatt on Value Investing

[Watch on YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=I6ued1LIdbM)

## Transcript

Renowned investors like Warren Buffett, Charlie Munger, and Joel Greenblatt emphasize that long-term financial success is rooted in personal integrity and disciplined valuation. They caution against deceptive accounting metrics like EBITDA and the "get-rich-quick" allure of active stock trading, which they liken to harmful addictions. Instead, these experts advocate for value investing, which involves buying quality businesses at a significant discount to their true worth to maintain a margin of safety. Beyond technical strategy, they stress that an individual's greatest asset is their own earning power and the habits they form early in life. Success also requires patience and the ability to ignore market volatility while waiting for "fat pitches" or clear opportunities. Ultimately, they argue that the high road of honesty is less crowded and provides a more sustainable path to wealth and personal fulfillment.
